What is Withering: Anemone?

Withering: Anemone is a dystopian 2d platformer game. You play as the spider Anemone, trying to save her forest from being consumed by the Wither, a blight-like plague consuming and corrupting the forest and the animals. Save the world by helping her get to the Tree of Essence, and heal its core with the Ember.
